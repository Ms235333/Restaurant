Food Ordering

Food ordering refers to the process of placing an order for food, typically through an online platform like a restaurant website or mobile app, where customers can browse a menu, select items, customize their order.Customers access food ordering through a restaurant's dedicated website or app, or through third-party food delivery platforms like DoorDash, Uber Eats, or Zomato. Users can explore a digital menu, view descriptions, and check prices before adding items to their cart.

Food catering is the business of preparing and serving food and drinks for events and other occasions. Caterers can work at remote locations, such as hotels, offices, restaurants, and other venues. Bulk Ordering
